Nexify Labs LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Bound mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our App.

By downloading, installing, accessing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, you must not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account: Email, name, and unique identifier from Sign in with Apple or Google.
  • Profile: Display name, age range, flow type, onboarding responses, and related preferences.
  • User content: Journal entries, closure letters, boundaries, mood check-ins, quiz responses, milestone descriptions, and similar content you create in the App.
  • Communications: Messages sent through chat and AI features.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device and app data: Device type, operating system version, app version, language, and general technical diagnostics.
  • Identifiers: Device identifiers, advertising identifiers (where permitted), and app instance identifiers used for analytics, security, and advertising measurement.
  • Usage data: Features used, screen views, interactions, session duration, taps, and in-app events (such as registration, paywall views, and subscription events).
  • Purchase data: Subscription status, product identifiers, and transaction-related metadata processed through Apple In-App Purchase. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.
  • Aggregated analytics: Statistical or de-identified usage information.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS, we may ask for permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites for advertising measurement. You can allow or deny this permission in the system prompt. If you deny tracking, we and our partners may still receive limited data for analytics and aggregated advertising measurement as permitted by Apple and applicable law.

Photos and Media

We may request access to your photo library for milestones, "Things I Love," or panic customizations. Photos are processed and stored locally on your device. We do not upload images to our servers; we store only local file paths needed for App functionality.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we collect and use a device push token to deliver reminders and service-related messages you opt into. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Deliver personalized coaching and AI-powered insights
  • Authenticate your account and manage subscriptions
  • Send service-related communications and optional reminders you enable
  • Measure App usage, performance, and feature effectiveness
  • Measure and optimize our advertising campaigns, including attributing installs, sign-ups, and purchases to ads
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, and security issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

We use service providers that process information on our behalf under contractual and legal obligations. These may include:

  • Supabase — Authentication and database hosting (Supabase Privacy)
  • OpenAI — AI/LLM processing for chat and analysis (OpenAI Privacy)
  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, In-App Purchases, and App Store distribution (Apple Privacy)

We do not sell your personal information for money. We and our partners (including Meta and TikTok) may collect and use app events such as app installs, app opens, account registration, paywall interactions, and subscription or purchase events to understand how users discover and use Bound and to measure the effectiveness of our ads.

On iOS, you can control tracking permission in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. You may also limit ad personalization through your device and platform settings.

Where applicable, we process personal data based on: performance of a contract (providing the App), legitimate interests (improving the App, security, analytics, and advertising measurement), consent where required (such as tracking permissions and optional notifications), and legal obligation.
